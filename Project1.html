VISITING THE BEAUTIFUL CITY OF BERLIN

Berlin is most known for being the capital of Germany. It is however also recognised as one of the global capitals for hipster young people. It is a celebrated tourist destination; people often visit Berlin to see all the museums and the historic sites. There are a number of sites that are of interest as far as the seperation of Berlin and Germany as a whole. Berlin is also recognised for being one of the cities that attract a lot of people in the tech world, such as startups. But more importantly, for the young, young at heart and the wild at least, Berlin is a city to uncover or discover fashion, food, and to just get your hipster on.

THINGS TO DO IN BERLIN

  • See the Reichstag
  • Go on a waking tour
  • Visit the monument for the murdered jews
  • Take a walk in the tiergarten
  • Discover some amazing graffiti
  • Take a trip to where the Berlin wall used to be

See The Reichstag

The Reichstag houses the German parliament, the Bundestag. The building has been in existence since the 1800s, and has had to be reconstructed a couple of times because of a fire among others. The beautiful edifice attracts a lot of tourists who come to take a lot of selfies, as well as well as, go up to the dome. From the dome one can see most of the whole of Berlin, as well as the parliament room below. Remember to book in advance before you get there, or else you will not be able to go into the building. Nevertheless, you can admire the edifice from outside. Remember to take loads of photos. Follow this link to get your tickets. www.bundestag.de

Go on a Walking Tour

Walking tours are very popular across the world now. It is very easy to find either a paid or a free walking tour in every touristic city. With the help of tour guide, you can see the most important sites in the city, as well as learn all the vital information and interesting stories surrounding these places. Among the places these tours usually visit, is the Brandenburger tor, which is somewhat the edifice that is most synonymous with Berlin.

Take a walk in The Tiergarten

Close the Brandenburger tor, and across the road from the Reichstag, is the Tiergarten. it is a very beautiful garden; full of trees, plants, water bodies, and monuments. It is a very serene place to take a walk, go running, walk your dog, or to just sit down and relax. For all you running freaks out there, this is definitely a good place to get your run on.

LINGUAPHILE LANGUAGE SCHOOL


Linguaphile Language School (LLS) is a school designed to provide high end language courses for people from different walks of life, and at different stages in their language learning. Do you need to learn a new language to be professionally more competitive? Do you need to improve your language proficiency? Are you just a linguaphile who seizes evry opportunity to learn a new language whenever you can? We got you! At LLS you can discover a world of language learning, that is very effective and well organised. Our curricullum is well developed, and can be compared to the very best out there. Our teachers are not only qualified, they are linguaphiles themselves and enjoy helping students on their journey to proficiency. We also have lessons for children. We offer lessons in the following languages:

  • English
  • French
  • Spanish
  • German
  • Italian
  • Chinese

For children we run courses such as:

  1. Afterschool lessons
  2. Weekend lessons
  3. vacation lessons

we also organise fieldtrips for students, where they can have the opportunity to practise speaking with native speakers of the language. Visit this website to learn more about the fieldtrips: www.rashingtontours.org
